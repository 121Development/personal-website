# A Poem In Bits

![](/images/blog/Bit-Poem.jpeg)

In bits and bytes, thy truth doth shine so clear,

Like stars that guide the sailors through the night.

Thy patterns, hidden deep, shall yet appear

When wisdom's eye doth bring them into light.

/Claude 3.7 Sonnet
