# Hungry AI

![](/images/blog/hungry-AI.jpeg)

AI is eating the software stack.

AI is also eating your content.

The sharp increase in AI-search and AI summaries for legacy search means drastically less traffic to your original content. Your content is scraped and served to the user without you getting a piece of the pie.

Will this stop? Probably not. How will we adapt? How will content creators counter or leverage this?

But maybe more importantly, what will be the next item on the menu for hungry AI?
